Fees

I’m a nervous speaker and I’ve taken the decision to only speak at events where I can justify taking the time out of client work to do it.

My standard speaking fee is £2,000 for a new talk on a topic we agree on, or £1,250 (plus VAT, travel, and accommodation) for one of my current talks. I'm willing to negotiate on my fee for free, community events.

Code of Conduct

I'm highly conscious of the need for conferences and meetups to offer a safe space for speakers and attendees alike, and therefore only speak at events that have a published and enforced Code of Conduct.

Biography

Anna is a freelance Front-End Developer based in London.

The longer version (52 words)

Anna is a freelance front-end developer based in London. She was awarded Young Developer of the Year by Netmag in 2013. She's written a book on Front End Style Guides, and co-hosts a pocast with Brad Frost on Style Guides. When she's not playing on them, she's researching the browsers on game consoles.
