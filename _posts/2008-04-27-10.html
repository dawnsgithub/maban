There’s a really sweet girl I babysit, and last night, she drew a picture of me. It took her about 15 minutes, and I was so happy with the result that I thought I’d post it up here.

So the chin may be a bit humungus, and the hair a little crazy, but she put so much care into doing it that I was really flattered. (I was expecting her to draw me with 2 noses, or a monobrow or something!)
